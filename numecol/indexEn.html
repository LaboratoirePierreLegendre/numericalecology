Laboratoire de Pierre Legendre

Pierre Legendre

Professor
Université de Montréal
Département de sciences biologiques

Postdoctoral fellow at the Genetiska Institutionen, Lunds Universitet, Sweden, in 1971-72. Research associate, then Research director at Université du Québec à Montréal, and finally Professor in the Département de physique. Moved to Département de sciences biologiques, Université de Montréal, in 1980.

Awards and Distinctions

Scientific prizes

  • Laureate of the 2019 Alexander von Humboldt Medal for Excellence in Vegetation Science of the International Association for Vegetation Science (IAVS), " for his valuable contributions in the field of numerical ecology".
  • Adrien-Pouliot prize for scientific cooperation with France, Association francophone pour le savoir (Acfas), October 22, 2015
  • Canadian Society for Ecology and Evolution President’s Award 2013
  • Laureate of the Canadian Council of University Biology Chairs Career Achievement Award, 2012.
  • Prix du Québec - Prix Marie-Victorin 2005
  • Laureate of the Twentieth Century Distinguished Service Award “for outstanding contribution to the synergistic development and direction of statistics, ecology, environment and society” awarded by the Ninth Lukacs Symposium “Frontiers of Environmental and Ecological Statistics for the 21st Century”, Bowling Green State University, Ohio.
  • Recipient of the Distinguished Statistical Ecologist Award of the International Congress of Ecology - (INTECOL) in 1994 and, in 1995, of the Romanowski Medal (environmental science) of the Royal Society of Canada
  • Recipient of the Michel-Jurdant prize for Environment Sciences, Association canadienne-française pour l'Avancement des Sciences (ACFAS), for 1986

Teaching prizes

  • Laureate of the Excellence in Teaching Award 2011 of the Faculty of Arts and Science, Université de Montréal, Science Sector.
  • Laureate of Université de Montréal Excellence in Teaching Award 2011, Full Professor category

Other distinctions

  • Web of Science Highly Cited Researcher in Environment/Ecology in 2001, then from 2014 to 2021
  • Member of the Order of Canada in 2021
  • Elected Corresponding Member of Academia Mexicana de Ciencias (AMC) in 2016
  • Officer of the National Order of Québec in 2007 (video)
  • Fellow of the Royal Society of Canada (Academy of Science) since 1992
  • Killam Research Fellow, Canada Council, in 1989-1991
